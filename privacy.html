Privacy Policy

This is the shortest privacy policy you'll ever read. Because we can't collect anything — even if we wanted to.

No accounts

No email, no phone number, no username, no password. You don't exist in our system.

No data collection

No analytics, no cookies, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting. We don't know who you are, where you came from, or what you did.

No message storage

Messages are encrypted end-to-end in your browser (ECDH P-256 + AES-256-GCM). The relay server holds only encrypted blobs in memory — nothing is written to disk. Close the tab and everything is destroyed.

No logs

The relay does not log IP addresses, connections, timestamps, or any metadata. There is nothing to hand over — even with a court order.

What the relay sees

An IP connected. An encrypted blob was stored. An encrypted blob was picked up. That's it. The relay cannot see who you are, what's in the blob, or who it's for. All of this exists only in memory and is gone when the connection ends.

Third parties

None. No third-party scripts, no CDNs, no external fonts, no APIs. Everything runs from our server and your browser.

Relay location

Helsinki, Finland, EU (GDPR). Hosted on Hetzner. Operated by Particular Ltd.

IP anonymity

For full IP anonymity, use Tor Browser. Voice notes may not work in Tor Browser.

Open source

Don't trust this page — read the code. The entire application is open source and fits in 522 lines.
